Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2025

Welcome to Lowry Bunks, an attendance management application designed for college students. Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Lowry Bunks may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, student ID, or other identifiers provided during registration.
  • Attendance Data: Records of attendance marked within the app.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, and app usage data for performance monitoring.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Maintain attendance records for users.
  • Provide analytics and insights for attendance tracking.
  • Improve app performance and user experience.

3. Data Sharing and Security

  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.
  • Data is stored securely using Firebase and is protected against unauthorized access.
  • Only authorized personnel can access certain data for maintenance and support.

4. User Control and Data Deletion

  • Users can request to update or delete their data by contacting support@lowrybunks.com
  • Uninstalling the app does not automatically delete your data; please reach out for complete removal.

5. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
